---
description: List the N most recent Leexi calls (default 10, max 100).
argument-hint: "[n]"
---

List the most recent Leexi calls. Default to `limit: 10`. If the user passes a number as argument (e.g. `/leexi-recent 25`), use that as the limit (cap at 100). Use `leexi_list_calls` with the limit and no other filter. Show as a compact table: time · title · participants · duration.
